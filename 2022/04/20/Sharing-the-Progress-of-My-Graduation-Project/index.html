HF-VINS

using Hierarchical-Localization in VINS-Mono

On the EuRoC dataset, performance is not intrinsically improved and remains almost identical to that of VINS-Mono. Since we are optimising the loop detection module, it is difficult to improve the overall performance unless we can detect loops that are not detected by VINS-Mono. Especially for the EuRoC dataset, traditional loop detection methods are sufficient. I think the real battleground for this system is the performance improvement under changing light conditions.

However, due to the epidemic in Shanghai, it is not yet possible to go out and collect data, so this experiment will be delayed for some time.

test on MH_03_medium

test on MH_05_difficult

However, we have made significant improvements in reducing the size of the offline maps and in the speed of saving and reading them. For the same dataset, map size was reduced to 1/3 and the time cost of saving and reading was reduced to 1/5.
